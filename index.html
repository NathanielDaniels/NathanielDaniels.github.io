About Me

Hey, my name is Nathan and I'm a Driven Self-Taught Web Developer from Chicago.

My main focus has been on Front-End Web Development. What really peaked my interest, was the ability to start with an empty slate and build anything you can imagine. It was also the Instant Gratification you get viewing your changes Immediately after making them. After I got started, I realized I was a Huge Fan of designing with CSS. Once I learned positions, I though I could do it all. Well, that was before I found out about Flexbox and Grid layouts... my Whole World Changed. Things became much clearer from that point forward, and my code, much cleaner.


Next, came the crazy adventure through javascript. Lots of mind blowing moments, especially while learning animations with JQuery. That's when I found my first coding obsession. I could tinker with animations for the rest of time! Though, thanks to a few clients, I started learning back-end to set up some forms. I had to quickly learn how to build a database to store data, then link it to my project. Now I have a working form at the bottom of this website that I set up myself.

Font-End: I have Solid knowledge of HTML and CSS/SASS. My current daily activities include becoming more proficient in Vanilla Javascript (ES6+) and JQuery. I am also fluent with NPM, GIT/Github and I'm very active with the Terminal. I also enjoy building HTML Emails.

Back-End: I have worked with PHP, Laravel and MySQL to add functionality to Forms. My Future Goals include becoming a Full-Stack Developer.


I am currently looking for a Full-Time Position in Either Front-End Web Development and/or HTML Emails.

If i'm not Deep into a coding project, you'll find me searching the internet for new ideas, fresh tutorials and tips and tricks. Soon I'll be writing blog posts and articles to showcase new ideas, or just my perspective. Even though I haven't been in this industry for very long, I strive to become a Name in Web Development.
